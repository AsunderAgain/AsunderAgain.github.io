World of Prompts

What sort of prompts?

They can be anything, mainly something fit for all ages that just stretches the mind and allows you to express your creativity. They can take place in a fantasy world or in our world, or not even a world. It could be a story based off the expression of a word and how it travels.

What’s the audience?

Those who enjoy writing or want to discover their love for it. It can also help those break from writer’s block, as prompts are just a beginning.

Can you submit your own prompts?

You will be able to submit your own prompts when prompted. Just email the contact email provided in the backmost tab of the website (the owner’s).

Can I submit my own work from these prompts given?

Similar to the last question, you can email it to the owner where it will be checked and given the A-OK before she submits it under the name you provided in the email. We also showcase some website where you can post your own work yourself!

Wattpad, for example, is a great and free website to not only post your work, but see millions of others' works as well.
